Learning how to implement a skill or knowledge is crucial as it bridges the gap between theoretical understanding and practical application. While knowing and believing in a concept provides a foundation, it's through implementation that one gains a deeper, hands-on comprehension. This active engagement allows individuals to troubleshoot, adapt, and refine their understanding. Knowing when to use a skill adds another layer of proficiency, as it demonstrates a strategic and context-aware application. Mere theoretical knowledge may lack the nuance needed to navigate real-world scenarios effectively.
